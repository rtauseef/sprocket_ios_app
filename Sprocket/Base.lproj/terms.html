HP SOCIAL MEDIA SNAPSHOTS TERMS OF SERVICE

While using this app you will be able to the Internet and use your Instagram, Facebook or Flickr account and create and print content of your choice.

Use of this app may require an Internet connection. Wireless performance is dependent upon your physical environment and your distance from an access point. Separately purchased data plans or usage fees may apply. Print times and connection speeds may vary. To print, you’ll need an Airprint™- enabled printer for iOS mobile devices, or an Android compatible printer for Android devices. The wireless connected printer and your mobile device must be on the same network to connect and print.

If you are using a data network as your Internet connection, you may incur additional data usage costs based on your data network plan.

To improve future versions of this app and HP printers, HP will periodically collect anonymous usage data from this app.
